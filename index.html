About Us

A humanitarian initiative built on presence, not promises.

Bloom Care Foundation is a humanitarian initiative dedicated to giving hope, care, and opportunity to children and communities in need.

Founded by Kaiser Andy Kofi (Certified Kaiser), the foundation focuses on supporting street children and underprivileged students by providing essential resources, mentorship, and encouragement to help them thrive.

Every visit is hands-on — from distributing food and clothing on the street, to walking into school compounds with rice, water, and stationery, to sitting with children in shelters late into the evening.

Founded and led by Kaiser Andy Kofi, Accra, Ghana
What Drives Us

Vision, mission, and the aim behind every visit.

Vision

A society where every child can flourish

To create a society where every child, regardless of background, has access to care, education, and the opportunity to grow and flourish.

Mission

Care that shows up, again and again

  • Support and uplift street children with food, clothing, and mentorship.
  • Visit schools with educational materials, sanitary products, and motivational guidance.
  • Inspire resilience and show children they can bloom into their full potential.
Aim

Consistency over spectacle

To be a dependable presence for children and communities in need — not a one-time gesture, but a foundation they can count on, visit after visit, school after school.

Founder

Kaiser Andy Kofi

Certified Kaiser · Founder, Bloom Care Foundation

Andy is a young visionary entrepreneur, creative director, and media personality building impactful brands, events, and opportunities for people across Ghana and beyond.

Bloom Care Foundation grew out of a simple conviction: that care shouldn't be occasional. Andy leads every outreach personally — from the first handshake on a school compound to the last child waved off at a shelter door.

"Every child deserves the chance to bloom — not despite where they started, but because someone showed up."
